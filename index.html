1 Introduction

Welcome to the LBYOMAT Course Book! This book is designed to guide students through the course by providing all necessary resources, materials, and instructions.

This course book is intended to ensure that DLSU Carlos L. Tiu-School of Economics students will be able to learn more about Mathematical Economics using R. You will find sections on the syllabus, course assessments, lectures, as well as guidance for navigating the course effectively.

1.1 About Me

My name is Jem Marie M. Nario, and I am once a lecturer of this course. I am excited to guide you through this journey of learning and discovery since I am also on a journey of learning and discovery while teaching part-time.

This book is a trial version which will be updated along the course as it also serves as a practice for me.
